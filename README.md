# Popcrush Project 2020
A free web collection of music from around the world.

_"I can understand wanting to have millions of dollars, there’s a certain freedom, meaningful freedom, that comes with that. But once you get much beyond that, I have to tell you, it’s the same hamburger." - Bill Gates_

## Composition
This collection is a blend of two music collections from the different time periods.

### Music from the period 2017-2019
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2017 until the end of 2019 (median year 2018). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned[^1], factually unmentioned[^2] or unheard of[^3], related artist[^4] or not: Post Malone (including his remix song with T-PAIN), Powfu, Kanye West, El Chombo, Drenchill, Maître Gims, Lily Allen, Dua Lipa, Collie Buddz, Childish Gambino, Ava Max, etc.
[^1]: Included in the song from the previous package of same domain, but not mentioned.
[^2]: Included in the song from the package of another content domain or same context, but not mentioned.
[^3]: Leading singer that was NEWLY added, missing in previous package of same domain.
[^4]: Not being mentioned anywhere in the song in which the related artist took part.

### Music from the period 2019-present
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2019 to present (median year 2020). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned[^1], factually unmentioned[^2] or unheard of[^3], related artist[^4] or not: 2 Chainz (factually unmentioned in Ariana Grande - Rule The World song), De Hofnar, James Foxx (including his song featuring T-PAIN), Kodak Black (previously unmentioned in T-PAIN - Florida Boy song), Lost Years, Mr. Talkbox (factually unmentioned in Bruno Mars - 24K Magic song), Tones and I, Travis Scott (factually mentioned in Travis Scott - Pick Up The Phone song), Victoria Justice, YoungBoy Never Broke Again, etc.
[^1]: Included in the song from the previous package of same domain, but not mentioned.
[^2]: Included in the song from the package of another content domain or same context, but not mentioned.
[^3]: Leading singer that was NEWLY added, missing in previous package of same domain.
[^4]: Not being mentioned anywhere in the song in which the related artist took part.
